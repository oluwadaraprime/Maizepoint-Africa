About Us

Maizepoint Africa is a startup that helps small scale maize farmers in Africa to prevent post harvest loss through the use of a simple machine and also helps to sell their produce in less than 24 hours after harvesting without having to leave their farms .

Our Vision

To be the leading agricultural platform that promotes the production, processing and sales of Maize in Africa.

Our Mission

To restore hope to maize farmers across Africa by creating an innovative and sustainable product that will help improve their productivity and standard of living.
